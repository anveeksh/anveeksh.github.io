Anveeksh Mahesh Rao — known as Ish — is a cybersecurity specialist, digital-forensics investigator, educator, and entrepreneur operating across the USA and India. He holds a B.Tech in Cyber Security & Cyber Forensics from Srinivas University and earned certifications including Cisco CCST, reinforcing a strong technical foundation.

He is currently pursuing his M.S. in Cybersecurity at Northeastern University (GPA 3.85), where his research explores cyber defence, privacy, decision-making, and critical infrastructure protection. His work connects advanced frameworks, AI-driven analytics, and human-centred security design to improve resilience across global digital ecosystems.

With a career spanning ethical hacking, penetration testing, VAPT, and secure operations, Ish bridges the gap between cutting-edge cyber threats and the practical needs of organisations, academic institutions, and individuals.

He is the Founder & CEO of niyantraX (formerly Cyber Tech Associates) — a comprehensive digital solutions and consulting organisation through which he has trained and mentored more than 10,000 learners, delivered workshops across India, and collaborated with universities and corporates to enhance cybersecurity readiness.

In addition to cybersecurity, Ish serves as the Managing Director of CBM Distributors and is the Founder of Mahesh Rao Group of Companies, operating across technology, education, and business development.
